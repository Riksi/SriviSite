How I can help you

    Whether you want guidance on grammar and style or in-depth feedback, I can offer services to suit your needs at reasonable rates and fast turn-around periods. Please contact me to discuss your particular requirements.

  • LIGHT/QUICK EDIT

    Check for consistent language, grammar, and punctuation, numbering of figures, tables, and citations in the manuscript.

  • MEDIUM EDIT

    All of the above, and in addition make suggestions to improve flow and readability, check for brevity, paragraph focus, and general organization and cohesiveness of the manuscript.

  • HEAVY EDIT

    All of the above, and in addition provide critical feedback on the scientific content as afforded by a peer-reviewer, make suggestions to present scientific results more effectively.

  • REVIEW RESPONSES

    Check if all comments in peer-reviews have been appropriately addressed; provide suggestions to improve effectiveness of author responses.

  • GRANT PROPOSALS

    Help formulate ideas into grant applications.
